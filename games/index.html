The Forest

The Forest (working title for now) is my attempt to merge a board game style interface with a narrative adventure. Think of FTL, Betrayal at House on the Hill, and any of the Fighting Fantasy books (but particularly Forest of Doom) mixed together.

You play as an ostracized druid charged with guarding a forest in the Taluskan Isles. All manner of creatures and mysteries lie therein and you must keep them contained whilst also dealing with a new problem; an apprentice that has been foisted upon you by the Auspex Council.

In terms of gameplay, you move around a hex grid as you navigate your way through the titular forest. There are many paths to follow and multiple encounters with branching dialogue, roleplay elements, and combat.

Anticipated Release Date: 2023

Platforms: iPad, iPhone, and Apple TV
